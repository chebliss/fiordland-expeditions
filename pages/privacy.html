Privacy Policy

Fiordland Expeditions Ltd. respects your privacy. This page outlines how we collect, use, and store personal information you share with us when enquiring about cruises, charters, or other services.

Information we collect. When you complete a contact or enquiry form, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, email address, phone number, and the message or trip details you've shared. When you visit our site, we also collect standard analytics data (pages viewed, referring URL, approximate location) via Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager.

How we use it. Enquiry details are used solely to respond to your enquiry and follow up about your booking. Analytics data is used in aggregate to understand site usage and improve the visitor experience.

Sharing. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Booking and payment processing may involve trusted partners (e.g. FareHarbor for bookings) under their own privacy terms.

Contact. For any privacy questions or to request deletion of your information, email info@fiordlandexpeditions.co.nz.

This policy is current as of 2026 and may be updated periodically.
