Your voyage

No fixed wake-up calls.

The pace is set by the fiord, the weather, and what you want from the day.

1

Day 1, PM — Departure: Manapouri to Doubtful Sound

Board at Manapouri. Pass through Wilmot Pass. Enter Doubtful Sound. Optional: fly direct to the helipad from Queenstown or Te Anau.

2

Bottlenose dolphins, fur seals, Fiordland crested penguins. Kayaking and swimming off the vessel available.

Transfer to Te Anau or Queenstown. Multi-night extensions available on request.

Milford vs Doubtful

Milford Sound is famous.
Doubtful Sound is different.

Milford Sound receives more than 800,000 visitors annually. Doubtful Sound is three times longer, with no road access to its inner arms. The difference is not aesthetic — it is structural. One sound has a car park at the head of it. The other requires a boat, a permit, and a skipper who knows where to go.
