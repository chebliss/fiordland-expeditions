Milford vs Doubtful

Milford Sound is famous.
Doubtful Sound is different.

Milford Sound receives more than 800,000 visitors annually. Doubtful Sound is three times longer, with no road access to its inner arms. The difference is not aesthetic — it is structural. One sound has a car park at the head of it. The other requires a boat, a permit, and a skipper who knows where to go.
