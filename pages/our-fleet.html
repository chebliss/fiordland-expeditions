The Original Expedition Workhorse

The Tutoko, our original vessel, is a 16m small ship with 8 guest berths — perfect for small groups of friends or the family.

Well laid out with two toilets, one shower, and a spacious lounge and seating area offering exceptional viewing from within. Kayaks for exploring, a dive compressor for scuba operations, and a selection of fishing equipment for catching an evening meal — all onboard.
