Bespoke Fiordland Experiences

Experience Unforgettable Private Charters

Our private charters offer a unique and tailored experience, ideal for families or small groups seeking adventure. Enjoy the stunning landscapes of Fiordland at your own pace, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

With a maximum of either 8 or 12 guests across our two vessels, our friendly, personable crew will do all they can to ensure your experience is the best we can make it. Our resident chef prepares the freshest seafood that you help to catch, for an evening feast fit for royalty.

Charter Options

What We Offer

🎣

Fishing Charters

Fiordland is revered for its fishing — blue cod, hapuka, bluenose, kingfish, trumpeter and more. Our crew know exactly where the fish are. Bring your own rods or use ours. The catch becomes dinner.

🛥

Private Vessel Charter

Take the entire vessel for your group. MV Tutoko II (12 guests) or MV Tutoko (8 guests) — fully crewed, fully catered, fully yours. Choose your fiords, your pace, your itinerary.

🤿

Dive Expeditions

Fiordland's underwater world is unique on the planet — black coral forests, rare marine life, and extraordinary visibility. Multi-day dive charters from consented anchorages in the remote fiords.

🦌

Hunting Expeditions

Red deer and Tahr hunting from remote Fiordland anchorages. Our crew know where the trophy animals run. A truly wild, remote, and rewarding experience for serious hunters.
