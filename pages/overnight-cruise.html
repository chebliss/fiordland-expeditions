Your itinerary

No fixed wake-up calls. No timed shore excursions.

The pace is set by the fiord, the weather, and what you want from the day.

Day 1
PM
Depart Manapouri
Scenic cruise across Lake Manapouri, coach over Wilmot Pass into Doubtful Sound. Optional: fly direct to the helipad from Queenstown.
Day 1
Eve
Explore the fiord
Cruise through towering cliffs, waterfalls, and hidden coves. Dolphins, seals, and penguins. Kayaking and fishing available.
Evening
Catch & cook dinner
Freshly prepared seafood dinner featuring the day's catch. Anchor in a sheltered cove — complete silence. Bioluminescence in summer.
Day 2
AM
Navigate the inner arms
Hall Arm, Crooked Arm, Bradshaw Sound. Wake to the peace of the fiord and a hearty breakfast while the vessel continues exploring.
Day 2
PM
Return to Manapouri
Return across Lake Manapouri. Transfer connections to Te Anau or Queenstown. Multi-night extensions available on request.
