The expedition

A landscape no other operator can take you to.

Four days in the UNESCO World Heritage waters of Fiordland National Park — Dusky Sound and the inner arms of Doubtful Sound — aboard the only vessel with a permit to take you there overnight. Led by professional photographer Derek Morrison, hosted by the operator with 25+ years on this water. Small group, working chef, deck-side helipad, no fixed wake-up calls.

Itinerary

Four days, set by the light.

No fixed schedule. The pace is set by the weather, the fiord, and the shot you came for.

Day 1
PM
Into the sounds
Te Anau pickup, helicopter into Dusky Sound, board MV Tutoko II for three nights. Welcome briefing and a first sunset on the water.
Day 2
AM / PM
Dusky exploration
Hidden coves, kayaks and tender shoots, golden hour from the deck. Evening editing and image critique with Derek aboard.
Day 3

Through the inner arms — Hall Arm, Crooked Arm, Bradshaw Sound. Places few photographers ever reach. Catch and cook dinner, anchor in silence.
Day 4

Final morning shoot, then Wilmot Pass back to Te Anau. Image review and farewell over lunch.
