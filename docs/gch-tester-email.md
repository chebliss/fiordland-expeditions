Thank you for offering to help us road-test the new **Guest Content Hub** —
the place our Doubtful Sound guests will eventually use to view their cruise
photos and (if they wish) share their own. It's still a Draft, so please poke
holes in it.

**The link**

  [https://STAGING-URL/gch](https://STAGING-URL/gch)

**What we'd love you to try (about 5 minutes)**

1. Open the link on your phone *and* on a laptop if you can — it should look
   tidy on both.
2. Click through the three tabs at the top: **Highlights**, **Your Gallery**,
   **Share Your Moments**.
3. On **Share Your Moments**, walk through all three steps:
   - Step 1 — pick a vessel, pick any cruise date, pop in any last name.
   - Step 2 — drop in 1–2 test photos (anything from your camera roll is
     fine — we'll wipe the test data afterwards).
   - Step 3 — the **Sharing Permissions** boxes are now ticked by default.
     Leave them ticked, or untick to see how that feels.
4. Hit **Submit** and check the upload status reads "uploaded · approved".

**What we'd love to know**

- Was it obvious what to do at each step, or did you get stuck?
- Did anything look broken, slow, or confusing on mobile?
- Anything in the wording that didn't sound like us?

A one-line reply is plenty — bullet points, screenshots, or voice notes all
welcome. No need to write an essay.

Anything you upload during the test goes into the Draft Airtable as
**approved** straight away (we've defaulted that on for this round so we can
see the full flow). We'll clear the test rows once we've reviewed your
feedback.
